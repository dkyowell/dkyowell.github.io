Our Story
Our Houston roots run deep.

We are a third generation family owned business that is passionate about bringing the best hardware and plumbing designs from around the world to Houston's design community.

Like many of his generation, our founder John Crosland headed West from Texas seeking work during the 1930â€™s. He found his way to Hollywood, California, and learned the overhead door trade. In 1938 he brought an entrepreneurial spirit and some Hollywood sizzle back with him to Texas, founding Hollywood Crawford Door Company in Houston. This was followed by Hollywood Steel, Hollywood Electronics, and Hollywood Builders Hardware.

We are proud to carry on this tradition, serving a new generation of Houstonians.
