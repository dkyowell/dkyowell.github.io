About Us

Our Roots in Houston Run Deep

Hollywood Hardware is an institution in the Houston home building industry. We are a third generation family owned business that is passionate about providing the best service to our clients.

Our founder John Crosland headed West from Texas seeking work during the 1930’s. He learned the garage door trade in Hollywood, California. In 1938, he brought his knowledge and an entrepreneurial spirit back with him to Texas, and founded Hollywood Crawford Door Company in Houston. This was the first of many “Hollywood” companies he founded in Houston: Hollywood Steel, Hollywood Electronics, and finally Hollywood Builders Hardware.
