About Us

Our Houston roots run deep! We are a third generation family owned business that is passionate about bringing the best hardware and plumbing designs from around the world to Houston's design community. We are equally passionate about providing quality customer service to our clients.

Like many of his generation, our founder John Crosland headed West from Texas seeking work during the 1930’s. He found his way to Hollywood, California, and learned the overhead door trade. In 1938 he brought an entrepreneurial spirit and some Hollywood sizzle back with him to Texas, founding Hollywood Crawford Door Company in Houston. This was the first of several “Hollywood” companies he started: Hollywood Steel, Hollywood Electronics, and finally Hollywood Builders Hardware.

We are proud to carry on this tradition, serving a new generation of Houstonians.
