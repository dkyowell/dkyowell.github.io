About Us

Our roots in Houston run deep! We are a third generation family owned business that is passionate about providing the best service to our clients.

Our founder John Crosland headed West from Texas seeking work during the 1930’s. He learned the overhead door trade in Hollywood, California. In 1938 he brought his knowledge and an entrepreneurial spirit back with him to Texas, and founded Hollywood Crawford Door Company in Houston. This was the first of several “Hollywood” companies he founded in Houston: Hollywood Steel, Hollywood Electronics, and finally Hollywood Builders Hardware.
